// Questions

Frequently
Asked

Are courses live or recorded?+
We offer both. Pro and Elite plans include live sessions with real-time interaction. All live sessions are also recorded so you never miss a class. The Starter plan gives access to recordings only.
Do I get a certificate on completion?+
Yes — all students who complete a course receive an EduPro Academy certificate. We're also partnered with industry bodies for additional co-certified programs that carry extra weight with employers.
How does placement support work?+
Elite plan students get full placement support: resume review, LinkedIn audit, mock interviews with industry professionals, and direct referrals to our hiring partners. We've placed students at TCS, Infosys, Amazon, and 100+ other companies.
Can I switch or pause my course?+
Pro and Elite students can pause for up to 30 days or switch courses anytime. Starter plan students can switch after completing 50% of their current course. We're flexible — life happens.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We accept all UPI apps (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), debit/credit cards, net banking, and EMI options via Razorpay. We also have institutional billing for corporate training programs.
Is there a money-back guarantee?+
Yes — 7-day no-questions-asked refund policy. If you enroll and decide it's not right for you within 7 days, we'll refund every rupee. No conditions, no forms to fill.
How are doubt sessions conducted?+
Doubt sessions happen on Zoom — twice a week for Pro students, daily for Elite. You can also post questions anytime on our community forum where instructors respond within 24 hours.
Do I need prior experience?+
Most courses start from the very beginning — no prerequisites needed. For advanced modules, requirements are clearly listed on the course page. We also have free foundation modules to get you started.

Still have questions?

Our team replies within 24 hours.
